About EcoWaste

EcoWaste is a platform connecting restaurants, grocery stores, bakeries, and individuals with people in need. Together, we're building a world where no meal goes to waste while hunger still exists.

How It Works

Simple steps to make a big difference

1

Donor Posts Surplus Food

Restaurants, stores, and individuals post available surplus food with details and pickup location.

2

System Notifies Recipients

Our platform instantly notifies nearby recipients about available food matching their preferences.

3

Recipient Claims and Picks Up

Recipients claim the food and coordinate pickup, ensuring fresh meals reach those who need them.
